To paint the sea

To paint the sea, only one person is needed;
But thousands for understanding its hidden properties.
They have wasted nights away,
Pens scratching nonstop,
Membranes of brain units
Devouring bubbles of logic.
The day has only begun.
The day has already ended.
What for, you say?
When the membranes merge
When the logics link
When properties are stated -
For what has become bigger
You see, they’re in ecstasy.

Beware of what’s there

Beware of what’s there
Covering up no-longers
Beware of the expected
Replacing eyes and ears
Beware, travelers
Your next step into meaning
Makes up eternal presence
An illusion thinner than mist
Floating among mountains
Your efforts will be exchanged
With color, recognition,
The forever loss of moments
I, yet to cross the gap, see
Red life piercing
Imposed geometries
And wonder about those
Who will look back at us.

Ah, shells of memes lying around

Ah, shells of memes lying around
Unseen for themselves
On lands infested by old thoughts
Even our eyes once were
Chiseled by shades of nutrition
Preferring to last just enough
She teases apart silk from friction
Singing the spindle in wooden fog
Behind her a robin rolls its throat
Drifting high, swift as the draft
Patterns multiplied on their own:
In twists, crosses, loops and paths
We are but the helping hand;
Ephemeral fingers of delivery.
