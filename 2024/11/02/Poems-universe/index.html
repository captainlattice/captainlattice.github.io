Good night, infinitesimal window

Good night, infinitesimal window
Everyone obliged
To love their time
Here drinking, working,
Pursuing profitable deaths -
None with an eye to observe the rate
By which deviants arise;
How dare they sing, laugh, chat, tangle like that?
The curse of being
Cures just by sight -
Thousands of young pens
Reaching out to the tide
Firm in their beliefs
Timid in their plan:
Sante! If not this time,
At least let us say so:
To artificial infinity
To but one shared life!

You

You are the untestable hypothesis
You are the space outside the brush
You are negation of the red track upon which we walked
If singers in the castle are released from the curse of cycles
Then you are the windows on its pinnacle.
I think of you and sing of you,
To tie in words that which cannot be compressed
Grand, microscopic,
infinitely lovable
Time beyond my time.

Wake up, wake up, wavelets

Wake up, wake up, wavelets
Weave what is yours
And cut what is not.

Neighborhood

I forget my name
In the new neighborhood
And become physical again:
Under soft things that change
Dwells the constant frame

Two shallow shovels
A ragged, dull knife
A goblet full of holes
The broken bow

What sleepy bird
Would stay in this bottomless cage?
Which dumb soldier
Would wear this topless helmet?

You may collect the smaller sticks
And make them into a wind-chime
Instead of hair pins or brushes.

These long ones are no good
For hooking, hitting, or piercing;
But do me a favor:
Turn them into a childâ€™s playthings.

Now that the handles are broken
And all strings have been cut
Pour wine into the goblet:
Dissolve what once claimed to be.

The Great Diamond

The Great Diamond, my mortal stars
Since when are you seven really afar?
The illogical, the furious, the self-blinded
All enlisted into a frictionless march on
Ominous glitter and crispy grass.
To the tower, to the tower, to the tower!
A cry stirring not a single icicle
Triggers, in me, fear: a heart of ice might
break sooner than later. Itâ€™s time
To rediscover a timeless grudge
And be knocked by bells to sleep:
A million pens silently sighed
Under the diamond, with no reverence:
Weaken my eyes or vacuum my heart.
I will nod as I fall apart.

Deadline

At night time again halts to a glide
The outside is mute and dark and void.
My room is the only kingdom
For talking to aliens on this phone
For making what has been made
Again and edit for all their ideas
Why are you painful, bright portal of hope?
If only I could clean the floor instead.
